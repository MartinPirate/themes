Our story

We build tools that make data feel obvious

Vantage started with a simple frustration: the people closest to the product were the furthest from the data. Dashboards went stale, questions waited days for a query, and gut-feel won more arguments than it should have.

So we built an analytics layer that sits on your warehouse, speaks plain English, and surfaces what changed before you have to ask. Today, thousands of teams use Vantage to ship with confidence.
